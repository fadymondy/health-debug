# Safe Mode Nutrition Logger

Boolean safe/unsafe meal tracking (no calorie counting). Predefined blacklist with trigger categories: IBS/GERD (eggs, falafel, fried foods, raw onion/garlic, cheddar), Gout (red meat, liver, duck, legumes), Fatty Liver (refined sugar, honey, nutella, jam, white flour, mixed carbs). Whitelist fast-logging: proteins (grilled chicken, white fish, cottage cheese, greek yogurt), carbs (oats, whole wheat toast, rice max 5 spoons), fats (olive oil, avocado). Critical system warnings on blacklist items.
