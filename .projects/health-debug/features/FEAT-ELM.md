# AI-Powered Meal Categorization & Food Expansion

Replace predefined protein/carb/fat picker with AI-driven meal analysis. When user logs food (custom or search), AIInsightEngine classifies it: macros, GERD/IBS risk, gout triggers, fatty liver risk, inflammation score. User can say 'Add mansaf' and AI classifies it automatically. Food database expandable via AI — no hardcoded lists required. Smart post-log message: 'Mansaf is high in fat — consider lighter portion given today's intake.'
