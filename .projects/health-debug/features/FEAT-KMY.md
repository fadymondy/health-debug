# Daily Flow Checklist

New 'Today' section at the top of Dashboard (or standalone card). Shows the day's health checklist: □ Morning hydration (250ml) □ Caffeine window open □ Meals logged (X/3) □ Stand sessions (X/6) □ Shutdown compliant □ Sleep goal met. Each item is a tappable glass row — tap opens the relevant detail page. Items auto-check based on real data. AI generates a daily morning briefing ('Today looks good — you're 2 glasses behind, consider...') shown at top. Progress ring shows overall daily score (0-100%).
