# Smart Drink Builder

Dedicated drink logging UI replacing the simple water buttons. Categories: Water, Herbal Tea (chamomile, anise, peppermint, ginger), Supplements (creatine, magnesium, vitamin C), Caffeine (delegates to CaffeineView). Each drink has: icon, volume picker (100-1000ml), smart note from AI ('Chamomile before bed reduces cortisol — good timing!'). Recent drinks shown as horizontal chip row. AI suggests next drink based on time of day and health state ('It's 9pm — chamomile or anise tea recommended'). Integrates with HydrationManager for water tracking.
